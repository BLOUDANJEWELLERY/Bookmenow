Our Story of Beauty & Excellence

Founded in 2008, LuxeStyle Salon began as a small beauty studio with a big vision: to provide world-class salon services in the heart of Kuwait City. What started with three styling chairs has grown into Kuwait's premier beauty destination.

Today, we serve over 200 clients weekly with a team of 25 internationally trained professionals. Our commitment to quality, hygiene, and customer satisfaction has made us the trusted choice for Kuwait's most discerning clients.
